Pitchfork Saturday Preview

Fri, Jul 17, 2009

Cymbals Eat Guitars 1:00 (A)

I really like the album “Why There Are Mountains”, especially “Share”. Looking forward to starting out Saturday watching this band.

Plants and Animals 1:45 ©

F*ed Up** 2:30 (A)

I’m excited to see these guys live. Should be the heaviest band of the day.

The Pains of Being Pure at Heart 3:20 ©

Final Fantasy 4:15 (A)

Yeasayer 5:15 ©

I really like the “All Our Cymbals” album, tribal chillout music.

Doom 6:15 (A)

Should be interesting, I like his stuff. I wonder if he’ll really show up, or if we’ll get a Doomposter

Beirut 7:25 © or Matt & Kim 7:30 (B)

I can’t decide on this one. Matt & Kim are fun band, but Beirut does some very interesting stuff too. I’m leaning toward Beirut now.

The Black Lips 8:30 (B) or The National 8:40 (A)

I will probably stick with The Black Lips, because I saw The National at Lollapalooza last year, albeit from far away saving a spot for NIN.
