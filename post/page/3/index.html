Singapore Arrival

Tue, Mar 21, 2006 singapore

We arrived in Singapore Saturday evening, after I slept for pretty much the entire flight from London. The airport was very nice, and very clean, out side there were palm trees and flowers, I could tell immeadiately how nice it was here. It was 35 degrees Celsius, about 95 Farenheit, when we arrived. It cooled off as the sun went down. The weather has been very nice, doesn’t feel too hot.

Frank Thomas

Tue, Feb 28, 2006 whitesox

I think Frank Thomas and Ken Williams both talk too much.
I will say that I would rather this happen now, than at the end of last season. It could have distracted from the great season the White Sox brought us last year. Dispite whatever Frank wants to say now, as a long-time fan, I’m glad he was a part of the 2005 White Sox, I’m glad I got to see him throw out the first pitch of the playoffs, and I’m glad I was able to see him hold up the World Series Trophy at the rally, after the parade.

Sox Fest 2006

Tue, Feb 7, 2006 whitesox

I attended SoxFest 2006, with my friends Brad, John J., and Jesse.
It was cool. We got to hear Ozzie Guillen, Ken Williams, and Hawk talk about the amazing 2005 World Series Championship season, and what is sure to be a great 2006. We also got to see an introduction to Jim Thome, and Rob Mackowiak, as well as new radio voice, and former Sox player, Chris Singleton. Thome should be a good addition if he is healthy next season, and Mackowiak should be a good utility player.

Airlink+ AWLL3025 on Ubuntu Linux 5.04

Thu, Jan 19, 2006 ubuntu, airlink, wifi

Note: I’m writing this mostly from memory, will go back and check, and update this to make sure it’s completely correct.
The Airlink+ AWLL3025 is a cheap 802.11g USB adapter.
Open Source Linux drivers for this adapter, however they aren’t usually included with any distro that I have seen, so you’ll need to compile them from source.
It seems the source files are not hosted on sourceforge anymore.
http://zd1211.ath.cx/ is the homepage for the zd1211 driver project.
